“One life, one encounter”

Every encounter is a once-in-a-lifetime encounter. Sometimes used as a reminder to cherish every moment because you’ll only experience it once.

“Beautiful person, thin life”

More superstition than anything else, this one really means that a “beautiful woman is destined to die young” but is more analogous to “beauty fades.”

“Fall down seven times, stand up eight.”

This one rolls “if at first you don’t succeed” and “perseverance is better than defeat” into one idiom.
